About First Med Care EMS

Dedicated to providing exceptional medical transport services to our community

Our Mission

At First Med Care EMS, our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and compassionate medical transportation services to patients in Stafford, TX and surrounding communities. We are committed to delivering the highest standard of pre-hospital care while ensuring comfort and dignity for every patient we serve.

Why Choose First Med Care EMS?

First Med Care EMS is dedicated to providing exceptional medical transport services with a focus on safety, reliability, and compassionate care. Our experienced team and modern equipment ensure the best possible outcomes for every patient.

Serving the Stafford Community

Located in the heart of Stafford, Texas, we are strategically positioned to provide rapid response times to medical emergencies and scheduled transports throughout the greater Houston area. Our state-of-the-art ambulances are equipped with the latest medical technology to ensure the best possible care during transport.
